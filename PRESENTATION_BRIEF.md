# DormChef Presentation Brief

## One-Line Pitch

DormChef is a social cooking app that helps people turn ingredients into practical recipes, share what they cook, and follow creators through a simple community-driven experience.

## Problem

Many people want quick, realistic meal ideas but face three issues:

- recipe apps often overwhelm users with long, impractical content
- social food apps inspire people visually but do not help them actually cook
- users want both discovery and utility in one product

## Solution

DormChef combines cooking utility with social interaction:

- AI recipe drafting from ingredients
- community recipe posts with photos, steps, and cook time
- saves, likes, ratings, comments, and follows
- step-by-step cooking mode with timers
- profile and notification systems that support creator discovery

## Innovation

What makes the app stand out:

- it bridges recipe utility and social sharing in one flow
- AI is used for practical recipe drafting, not just novelty
- cooking mode turns a social post into an actionable guided experience
- the product is lightweight and focused instead of trying to be a giant generic recipe database

## Competitor Comparison

Compared with generic recipe apps:

- DormChef is more social and creator-driven
- content is faster to browse and easier to engage with

Compared with food social platforms:

- DormChef is more useful for actual cooking
- posts are structured around ingredients, steps, time, and budget

## Key Features To Mention

- email-verified authentication
- AI recipe generation
- recipe post creation and editing
- save and like system
- follows and public profiles
- comments and ratings
- push notifications
- cooking mode with timed steps

## Suggested 3-Minute Structure

1. Problem
2. Solution

6. Closing value statement

## Closing Line

DormChef is not just a recipe app and not just a food social feed; it is a practical cooking platform that helps users discover, create, and actually cook.
